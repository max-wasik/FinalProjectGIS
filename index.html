Dangerzones in Graz

An interactive PPGIS Project

Created by Maximilian, Patrick, Emilia and Lena

Welcome to our interactive StoryMap of Graz! This project guides you through the city’s ten most iconic attractions which are places that reflect the rich cultural heritage, architectural diversity and vibrant urban life of Austria’s second-largest city. From Renaissance buildings in the historic Old Town and baroque palaces to modern art museums and lively markets, Graz offers a unique blend of history and contemporary culture.

As you scroll, the map will automatically follow your journey, zooming in on each landmark to give you a deeper geographic sense of how the city is structured. Whether you're planning a visit, exploring for academic purposes, or simply discovering new corners of Graz, this StoryMap provides a visual introduction to the most important sites that shape the character of the city.

Conclusion

This StoryMap highlights the ten most popular places in Graz and shows how geography, architecture, and cultural heritage are connected within the city. By combining interactive mapping with a narrative structure, our website aims to make spatial information intuitive and engaging. The project demonstrates how urban exploration can be transformed into a dynamic digital experience. Graz offers an exceptional variety of culture, architecture, and history. Come to Graz and experience them yourself!

Thank you for exploring Graz with us!
